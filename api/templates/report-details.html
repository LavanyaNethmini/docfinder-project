Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about reporting found documents

You can report any official identification documents including national IDs, driver's licenses, passports, academic certificates, bank cards, insurance cards, and other important personal documents. If you're unsure whether something should be reported, it's better to report it than not.

Our system automatically detects and obscures sensitive information in uploaded images. For extra protection, you can:

  • Place a finger over ID numbers when photographing
  • Use photo editing tools to blur sensitive details
  • Only provide the last few digits of any numbers in the description

We offer several contactless return options:

  • Drop-off Locations: Leave the document at one of our verified partner locations
  • Mail Return: We can provide prepaid shipping labels (fees may apply)
  • Third-Party Pickup: Arrange for a trusted intermediary to handle the exchange
