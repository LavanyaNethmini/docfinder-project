Our Mission

Every year, millions of important documents are lost worldwide, causing stress and inconvenience. DocFinder was created to solve this problem by connecting people who find documents with those who lost them.

We believe in the power of community and technology to make the world a better place, one returned document at a time.

Our Team

Founded by a group of university students who kept losing their IDs, DocFinder is now maintained by a dedicated team of developers and community managers.

We're passionate about creating solutions that make a real difference in people's lives through simple, effective technology.

How DocFinder Works

Our simple three-step process makes it easy to reunite lost documents with their owners while maintaining privacy and security.

1. Report or Search

Report a found document or search for your lost one in our secure database.

2. Secure Matching

Our system verifies ownership while protecting personal information.

3. Reunite Safely

Arrange a safe return through our verified contact system.
