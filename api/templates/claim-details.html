Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about claiming your document

Most verifications are completed within 2-4 hours during our standard business hours (9AM to 6PM, Monday through Friday). More complex cases or verifications initiated outside business hours may take up to 24 hours. You'll receive email notifications at each stage of the process.

If we're unable to verify your claim, you'll receive a detailed explanation of which verification steps need additional information. You can then:

  • Submit additional documentation
  • Try an alternative verification method
  • Contact our support team for assistance

The basic verification service is completely free. However, some return methods may have associated costs:

In-Person Pickup

Free of charge

Verified Drop-off

Free at most locations

Secure Shipping

Charges apply based on destination

Any applicable fees will be clearly displayed and require your approval before processing.
